Attractions

There are many wonderful attractions available to you here in The Domincan Republic! Bavaro beach is the main beach in Punta Cana. It stretches 30 miles across the caost of Punta Cana. Bavaro beach has many exiting things to like beach bars, resorts, restaurants, and activites to do along the way. This is a public beach allowing anyone in Punta Cana to come and enjoy the beatiful weather and ocean! Alto De Chavon is another sight to see. It is a 16th Centruy Mediterranean village that was built of enitrely stone. The villages was built over six years and lays 300 feet above the Chavon River. The village holds a giant 5,000 seat amphitheater and many shopping areas. Columbus Park is considered one of the most "active parks" in the Domincan Republica dn faces the oldest cathedral in the Americas. At the center of the park sits a larve statue of Christopher Columbus which points to the island he desovered. There are many musicians and sidewalk cafes along the way.

More Attractions

There are many more attractions worth viewing if you have the time. The Columbus Lighthouse is located on the easter end of Santo Domingo. The building was built in the shape of a cross and holds the remains of Christopher Columbus. The building was built ot commermorate the 500th anniversary of Columbus' arrival. The museum contains many interesting artifacts from around the Americas. Looking for more water related attarcions? Perfect, El Astron is a massive 394 foot long freighter from Russia! It crashed while carrying tons of corn to Cuba in 1978. The freighter split in two and sunk to the ground of Bavaro. El Astron is a popular site recommended for advanced divers. The last attraction is the Ocean World Adventure Park. Ocean World is located along Cofresi Beach about the miles from central Puerto Plata. The park is open Tuesday to Sunday, from 8:30 am. to 5:30 p.m. The park offers many things to do like reef snorkeling, animal shows, access to Doolphin beach, lunch, and transportation from hotels. Cost start at $69 for adults, $54 for seniors 65+ and kids 4-12, and infats 3- can enter for free!
